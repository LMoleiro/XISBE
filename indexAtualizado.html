ABOUT US

The SBE – Semana da Bioengenharia - is a week-long event dedicated to showcasing the exciting world of Bioengineering. It combines biology, biomechanics, artificial intelligence, pharmaceuticals and technologies, and innovation to address real-world challenges.

WHO is it for?

The SBE is designed for students, researchers, and enthusiasts of Bioengineering . Whether you're curious about groundbreaking discoveries, eager to network, or simply exploring your passion for the field, this event has something for everyone

WHAT can you expect?

- Inspiring talks by leading experts

- Hands-on workshops to build practical skills

- Opportunities to network with professionals

- Insights into career paths and the future of Bioengineering.

WHERE does it happen?

Instituto Superior Técnico (IST), Lisbon
